title: Designation
tags: [terms]
---

Many robotkin will take on a **designation**, an identifier used in place of, or in addition to, a "human" name. This is particularly common amongst [drones] and [dolls].

Designations typically consist of a mixture of English language letters and numbers. They may also include symbols and letters from other alphabets. They may further be inspired by technology limitations, such as being written in hexadecimal or resembling the output of a [hash function].

Designations are usually meaningful to the individual it represents, incorporating personally significant digits and symbology.
