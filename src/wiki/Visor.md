A **visor** is a form of headware that obscures the wearer's face. They are commonly, though not exclusively, worn by [drones].

In the robotkin community, this often takes the form of a smooth, opaque helmet that covers much or all of the head. They may be adapted from gas masks or medical face shields.

Advanced visors may incorporate electronic displays to display graphics on the front of the visor, or microphone and speaker set ups to amplify the wearer's voice.
