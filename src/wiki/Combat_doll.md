**Combat dolls**, also referred to as **tactical dolls**, are a subset of [dolls]: feminine [androids] designed for combat purposes. They are typically depicted as agile, tactically minded, and proficient with firearms or other weaponry.

The controller of a combat doll is often referred to as a "wielder", with the doll as their weapon.

[dolls]: {{ 'doll' | pageUrl }}
[androids]: {{ 'android' | pageUrl }}

## In popular culture

### In media

The T-Dolls from the _Girls' Frontline_ video game series are the most prominent example of the combat doll archetype in mass media.

## In the robotkin community

Combat dolls, and dolls generally, are considered part of the robotkin community by virtue of their self-identification as machines.
