**Dolls** are a subset of feminine [androids] that are designed with a specific purpose, such as service or combat, and serve an owner or controller.

They share some overlap with [drones], however dolls are usually unique and do not intend to obfuscate their identities in the same way that drones do.

[androids]: {{ 'android' | pageUrl }}
[drones]: {{ 'drone' | pageUrl }}

## Combat dolls

Combat dolls, also referred to as tactical dolls, are a subset of dolls designed for combat purposes. They are typically depicted as agile, tactically minded, and proficient with firearms or other weaponry.

The controller of a combat doll is often referred to as a "wielder", with the doll as their weapon.

## Service dolls

Service dolls are a subset of dolls designed for domestic service, such as cooking and cleaning. They are often depicted wearing black and white French maid clothing.

## In popular culture

### In media

The T-Dolls from the _Girls' Frontline_ video game series are the most prominent example of the combat doll archetype in mass media.

## In the robotkin community

Combat dolls, and dolls generally, are considered part of the robotkin community by virtue of their self-identification as machines.
