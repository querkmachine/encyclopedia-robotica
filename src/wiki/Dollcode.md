A **dollcode** is a form of [designation] that is used by [dolls].

It consists of a series of rectangles, vertically subdivided into upper and lower halves, each capable of dispying a trinary (3-bit) value.

A doll's numerical designation may be converted into a dollcode for visual representation using a variety of bases.

For example, the designation '12345' may be converted using base 10 to create the dollcode ▖▘▖▘▖▌▌▖▌.
