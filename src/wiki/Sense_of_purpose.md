---
title: Sense of purpose
tags: [concepts]
---

Many robotkin are drawn to the identity by a **sense of purpose** that can be attained by being a machine.

Machines in real life are designed and built to perform, and normally excel at, a specific function or set of functions. These can range from daily household tasks, to complex operations, to defense, to simple entertainment.

Having a sense of purpose can help robotkin individuals feel grounded in themselves and what they exist to do. Euphoria can be derived from fulfilling that function well.
